From data-centric to user-centric

Stratio has a big challenge: democratize access to the most complex technologies for business people with no specific technical education. With that goal in mind, we design a system as little design as possible, focused on the essentials. This approach let us guide the users subtly across the use of our products, in a natural way, so that they can focus their mind on what they want to do, not how to do it.
